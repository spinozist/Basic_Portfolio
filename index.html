About Me

I am currently a Senior Planner in the Research and Analytics Division at the Atlanta Regional Commission (ARC), where I am responsible for data analysis, visualization, and outreach in the futherance of planning objectives for the Atlanta Metro region. Prior to my career in city and regional planning, I taught Geography for a number of years at Ivy Tech Community College and Indiana University Purdue University Columbus (IUPUC) in Indiana.
