About Me

I am currently a Senior Planner in the Research and Analytics Division at the Atlanta Regional Commission (ARC), where I take the lead on a number of data analysis, visualization, and outreach projects. Prior to my career in city and regional planning, I taught Geography in Indiana at Ivy Tech Community College and Indiana University Purdue University Columbus (IUPUC).
